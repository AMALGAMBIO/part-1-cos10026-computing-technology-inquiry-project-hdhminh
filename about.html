Our Works

Yugen

Yugen is an RPG-styled rhythm game that blends the thrill of musical precision with deep role-playing mechanics.
Players embark on an epic journey, mastering rhythmic combat to the beat of a dynamic soundtrack.
With immersive storytelling, customizable characters, and a unique fusion of music and strategy, Yugen delivers an unforgettable experience for rhythm and RPG enthusiasts alike.

Beautiful World - 美しい世界

Beautiful World is a game that explores the clash between two factions, each with a vision for the future of humanity.
One faction, having abandoned Earth, seeks to establish a new civilization on a distant planet, controlling all means of interstellar travel.
The opposing faction, a rebellion group, fights to return to Earth, believing that humanity's true destiny lies in restoring their home planet.
With strategic combat, deep narrative choices, and a richly immersive world, Beautiful World challenges players to decide the fate of civilization.

Berry Sauce - ベリーソース

Berry Sauce is a psychological horror game that follows the journey of a protagonist returning to an abandoned berry field from their childhood.
As they search for the hidden secrets of their past, the memories of the once-thriving land begin to resurface—twisted and distorted by time and trauma.
With unsettling atmosphere, mind-bending puzzles, and haunting visuals, Berry Sauce challenges players to uncover the truth behind the protagonist’s dark past.

MIRROR

Set during the height of the French Revolution, MIRROR is a drama-driven game where every choice shapes your fate.
As an ordinary 20-year-old, you must navigate the chaos of revolution—choosing to escape its horrors or rise to power within it.
With branching narratives, morally complex decisions, and historical intrigue, MIRROR immerses players in the turbulent era where loyalty, ambition, and survival collide.

HMS Captain

HMS Captain is an open-world pirate adventure where you take command of your own ship and carve your path across the vast seas.
Explore uncharted territories, engage in naval battles, and uncover hidden treasures while embracing the life of a pirate.
Set sail freely to wherever the winds take you, but always be wary—the Royal Navy is never far behind, ready to hunt you down.
Live a life of freedom, plunder, and discovery in this immersive high-seas experience.
